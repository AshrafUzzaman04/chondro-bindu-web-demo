Chandrabindu Student Consultancy is one of the largest and leading student consultancy firm in Bangladesh. We operate a full-time Education Counseling Service through our office in Dhaka , in order to help students choose and plan their higher education and career needs wisely and implement decisions about their future in a well-planned manner. We provide guidance and information about the various faculties and universities around the world. We provide detailed relevant information on costs, program structure, course outcomes etc. to allow students to compare programs and decide which suites them best. In this striking universe, there is no limitation of knowledge & there is no royal way to learning. Still we are learning & innovating something new. Our ultimate target is to proper assist to build up students, immigrants, travelers & workers for better higher studies, settlement, workplace after that they can spread up knowledge all over the respective position as a proper skilled person. Our friendly & approachable staffs are committed to providing high levels of support & supervision.

 

 

 



USA has the world’s largest international student population, with over 800,000 students choosing to broaden their education and life experience in the United States...............

Australia is currently the third most popular destination for international students in the English-speaking world, behind the United States and the UK. Many international students choose to study there because of the cultural diversity, friendly natives, and high quality of education.....
 

Out of all the many choices available to students, Canada comes out on top. In fact, the number of international students has risen dramatically over the last few years.....		  

The United Kingdom of Greet Britain and Northern Ireland, commonly known as the United Kingdom or Britain. They also have a reputation for world-class research. UK higher education degrees and qualifications are recognised by employers and academics worldwide.......


Universities in Denmark are known for their thriving learning environment and high standard education. The strengths of the teaching and learning style of Danish universities lie in the problem-based learning methods that encourage students to find their own solutions and become innovative thinkers...

With an excellent education system, internationally accredited qualifications, ample research opportunities and a matchless quality of life, New Zealand has a lot to offer. While studying here, you can be assured of a welcoming and friendly society with an excellent support system for international students...
 

 Estonia, a country in Northern Europe, borders the Baltic Sea and Gulf of Finland. Including more than 1,500 islands.The country boasts incredible scenery, stunning architecture, and offers a classic European experience....		  

Studying in Russia is a popular choice for international students with a wide variety of interests, be they MBBS, literature, art, history, or Russian language studies. 


 More and more students each year are choosing to study abroad in Poland. These students are drawn to its high-quality education institutions, low tuition fees, and equally low cost of living....

Studying in South Korea will open up a wide range of employment opportunities for you. Graduates from Korean universities are highly sought after by employers all over the world....
